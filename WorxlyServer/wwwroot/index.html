Worxly is a cross-platform solution designed to bridge the gap between skilled workers and customers who require their services. The system includes a mobile application, server infrastructure, and database hosted on Azure. Leveraging modern technologies like Avalonia, ASP.NET, and MS SQL Server, Worxly offers a seamless and scalable platform for service matching and management.
